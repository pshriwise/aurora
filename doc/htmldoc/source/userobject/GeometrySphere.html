      GeometrySphere

      Snap nodes to the surface of a sphere on adaptivity

      Whenever the mesh changes all nodes on the supplied boundary are snapped to the surface of the sphere specified by center and radius. This allows adaptive refinement of curved geometries.

      Input Parameters

        C++ Type:std::vector<BoundaryName>

        Options:

        Description:List of boundaries whose nodes are snapped to a given geometry

        Default:TIMESTEP_END

        C++ Type:ExecFlagEnum

        Options:NONE, INITIAL, LINEAR, NONLINEAR, TIMESTEP_END, TIMESTEP_BEGIN, FINAL, CUSTOM, ALWAYS
